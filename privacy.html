Privacy, in plain English

Last updated: July 15, 2026. This notice describes the website that is live today. Production client services will receive their own data map and disclosures before activation.

The interactive preview

Both workflow previews run entirely in your browser. Your answers are not transmitted, logged, or stored. Closing or refreshing the page clears them. The preview does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other personal information.

The Scout preview does not search for real people or businesses. Its result is an illustrative research plan. The Qualify preview uses fixed decision rules rather than an external AI service.

This website

  • We set no analytics or advertising cookies of our own.
  • We run no third-party analytics or advertising trackers.
  • The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub receives standard web-server request data such as IP address and user agent; see GitHub's privacy statement.

Contacting us

If you use the email link, your email application sends the information you choose to include directly to ReeMergence Holdings. We use it to respond to the inquiry and maintain the resulting business conversation. We do not sell that contact information.

When online scheduling is introduced, the booking step will identify the scheduling provider and link to its policy before the integration is enabled.

Future Scout and Qualify services

A production engagement may involve client-authorized public research sources, CRM records, advertising platforms, scheduling tools, or messaging providers. Those connections are not active on this website. Before a client workflow is enabled, its service agreement and operating record should identify:

  • the sources and providers involved;
  • the fields collected, their purpose, and retention period;
  • who can access, correct, export, suppress, or delete records;
  • which decisions remain human-approved; and
  • the applicable opt-out and do-not-contact process.

Stopping contact

If ReeMergence Holdings contacts you commercially, you may ask us to stop in plain language. The request will be recorded in the appropriate suppression list so the same company or address is not treated as a new prospect later.
